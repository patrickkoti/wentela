  2. About Dr. Ng'ang'a

Dr. Ng’ang’a is experienced in Customs and business tax management. Having served for over 30 years in the customs department and management of various businesses. He is also a theologian and lecturer on customs administration. He is head of operations and deputizes the chairman. He is a graduate from St Paul University Limuru with the following Academic Qualifications

Bachelor of Business Administration and Management
Master of Business Administration, Finance option from Kenya Methodist University,
Doctor of Business Administration in Entrepreneurship from Both Walden University and Atlantic International University.

He is Tax Professional and a Master Trainer accredited by East African Community and Kenya Revenue Authority. His experience in Tax matters and Customs Administration spans for more than 30 years. He is a subject matter expert on Harmonized System Nomenclature and Customs Law and Practice. He Lectures at Kenya school of Revenue Administration in Master’s program and Post Graduate Diploma in Customs Administration.
